About This Project

In a world of quick texts and fleeting social media posts, we wanted to create something more intentional. Something that takes a moment to create and feels special to receive.

Virtual Postcard Platform was born from a simple idea: what if we could combine the thoughtfulness of a handwritten postcard with the creativity of a coloring book and the convenience of digital sharing?

Whether you're thanking a friend, encouraging a classmate, or just letting someone know you're thinking of them, postcards create a pause in our busy lives to connect meaningfully.
